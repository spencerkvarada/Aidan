About Me

I was ten years old when I completed my first public speaking class. Later, I started competing at a national level. I was a finalist at regional and national tournaments as well advancing to the Team Policy Debate final round two years in a row at the National Invitation Tournament of Champions. During this time, I competed in every style of speech and debate while learning to love the process of crafting and perfecting speeches for competition. Over the last 3 years since graduating, I have taught numerous classes to elementary, middle, and high school students on basic and advanced public speaking. Teaching students that through the building of confidence, comes humility and focus.
